Lost Mine Trail

This trail is located in Big Bend National Park, TX.

It is somewhat long to hike and reach to the top, but really worth it! As you elevate more you can feel the wind blow harder and fresher, it's very nice. Watch out for mountain lions and black bears!(very rare)
Santa Elena Trail

This trail is also located in Big Bend

It's the hottest trail in Big Bend! You need lots of water for this one. As you get deeper into the trail scream your name or something and hear the elegant echo through the canyon.
Mount Bonnell

Quite an easy trail to hike located in Austin, TX. It's only like 100 steps I think.
Go just in time to see the sunset, it's worth it!
Chisos Basis Campground

Wonderful place to camp!

A lot of space for your personal tent and automobile. Take a lot of flashlights and lamps it gets very dark and scary at night. But relaxing from all the stars in the sky!
